CHEAP

The Bus

Sure, it's only a few dollars. But it runs on its own schedule, drops you wherever the stop is, and isn't built for groups, events, or getting home safely after midnight.

THE SWEET SPOT

Eazy Way Shuttles

Direct zone pickups. Timed to your event. Built for groups of 8–15. Starting at $30 one-way / $60 roundtrip — way less than a rideshare split five ways or a party bus rental.

Uber, Charters & Party Buses

An Uber from Ann Arbor to Detroit can run $60+ — for one person. A party bus rental starts around $150/hour. Great if you can afford it, overkill if you can't.

We're not trying to beat the bus on price. We're the easier, safer, more organized way to travel together — without paying charter-bus money to do it.
